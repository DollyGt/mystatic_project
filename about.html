WHO WE ARE

Naledi's Concierge Services was launched in Maun in November 2016 by Co-founders Naledi Seitsang and Tsame Seitsang. From last minute restaurant reservations, holiday bookings and festival tickets, to access to clubs, parties and special events, Naledi's Conciege Services quickly became a house hold name in Maun.

We never looked back since. We like to think of ourselves as working "with", not "for" our clients, a philosophy that allows us to fully understand and meet your needs whilst creating a friendly yet professional relationship. our focus is on providing a service that makes your life easier, more efficient and economical. You can depend on us to provide completely reliable service you can trust.

We offer you unrivalled privileges across every chapter of the luxury lifestyle industry. Whether it's a priority reservation at a five starred restaurant, the Best Available Rate at a five star hotel in anywhere in the country or cocktails on arrival at Maun's most glamorous nightspot, we have got your back.

Whether you are a thrill-seeker looking for extraordinary adventure or prefer to indulge in the most lavish and divine experiences, our network of contacts througout the country enable us to make dreams a reality.

From the rare and extraordinary to the irresistible and divine, we pave the path to unique and inaccessible experiences money can't buy. Let us handle whatever is stressing you out, so you can have your cake and enjoy your extra time eating it too.
